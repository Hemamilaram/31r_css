About Antoine’s



Tradition since 1840


Welcome to Antoine’s, one of the oldest family-run restaurants in the United States, proudly serving the heart of New Orleans since 1840. I’m proud to say that Antoine’s has a deep-rooted history in this vibrant city, founded by my ancestor, Antoine Alciatore, a French immigrant with a passion for bringing authentic French dining traditions to New Orleans.


One of our most famous creations is the Oysters Rockefeller, a dish invented right here in 1850. It’s still made today with our signature blend of spinach, herbs, and breadcrumbs, baked to perfection. It’s a taste of history on a plate! We also take pride in our Filet de Boeuf en Croûte, a mouthwatering beef tenderloin wrapped in puff pastry, and our Pompano en Papillote, a delicate fish dish that’s been a favorite for decades.
